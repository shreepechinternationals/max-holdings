Our Mission

Max Holding Private Ltd aims to invest in and build a portfolio of sustainable investments, driven by established goals to build shareholder value, mindful of its social, economic and environmental responsibilities, as it remains to be committed to integrity, honesty and fairness in all dealings with its stakeholders.

Our Vision

Our vision is to contribute to the sustainable development of Nepal economy by building market-leading businesses through our focused approach. Professional integrity in all our financial and business matters constitutes one of our core strengths.

Why Choose US

Max Holdings is based on a tactical and proven combination of investment planning, opportunity assessment, project initiation, execution and management, introduction of innovative management techniques and the creation of successful partnerships with multinational corporations.
